HereMinder Privacy Policy

Last updated June 27, 2026

HereMinder is built to keep your data yours. The short version: your shopping list and everything HereMinder learns stays on your iPhone. There are no ads, no trackers, and we never sell or share your information.

Your list stays on your device

The items you add are stored locally on your iPhone. If you turn on HereMinder Pro sync, your list is stored in your own iCloud account through Apple's services so it appears on your other devices. We never receive or store your list on our servers.

Location

HereMinder uses your location on your device to remind you when you arrive at a relevant store. To find nearby stores, an approximate location is sent to Apple Maps. We do not store your location or link it to you.

Learning your stores

To suggest the stores you actually shop at, HereMinder can note the stores you pin and, if you leave the option on, the places where you stop and shop. This learning happens entirely on your iPhone. It is never uploaded, shared, or used for anything other than improving your own reminders, and you can turn it off at any time in Settings.

On-device intelligence

On supported iPhones, HereMinder uses Apple's on-device models to understand items you type and to tell what kind of store a place is. This runs entirely on your iPhone. No list items, store names, or prompts are sent to us or to any server.

No ads, no tracking, no selling data

HereMinder contains no advertising and no third-party tracking. We do not sell or share your information with anyone.

Purchases

Purchases such as HereMinder Pro are processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment details.

Children

HereMinder is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
