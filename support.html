Reminders aren't firing when I arrive at a store

HereMinder needs your location permission set to Always so it can remind you while the app is in the background, which is when arrivals usually happen. Open the iOS Settings app > HereMinder > Location, and make sure it's set to Always and that Precise Location is on. Arrival reminders also rely on iOS geofencing, which can take a short while to register a new place and works best with a good GPS signal.

How does HereMinder know what store sells my item?

When you add an item, HereMinder tags it with the kind of store it's usually sold at — milk at the grocery store, a hammer at the hardware store. Big-box stores like Walmart or Target remind you of your whole list in one trip. On iPhones with Apple Intelligence, an on-device model helps tell ambiguous stores apart; on other iPhones it uses Apple Maps place types and a built-in list of well-known brands.

An item was tagged for the wrong kind of store

Tap the item and adjust which store types it belongs to. HereMinder remembers your correction and prioritizes it next time, so teaching it once sticks.

What's included free, and what's in Pro?

HereMinder is free to use, and every new install gets full Pro for 14 days so you can feel it work in your routine. After that, the free tier keeps the everyday essentials, including store-type sorting and custom stores and rules. HereMinder Pro is a one-time purchase (no subscription for individuals) that unlocks unlimited active reminders, iCloud sync across your devices, multiple lists, unlimited pinned places, and alphabetical and manual sorting.

I bought Pro but don't see it on my other device

Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID, then open HereMinder's settings and use Restore Purchases. iCloud sync also needs to be turned on for your list to appear across devices.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your list stays on your iPhone, there are no ads or trackers, and nothing about your list or your stores is sent to us. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.
